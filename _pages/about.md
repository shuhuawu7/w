A Short Bio:
Shuhua Wu is a Ph.D. student in Information Systems Management at the Fox School of Business, Temple University. Prior to joining Temple, she earned a master’s degree in Business Analytics and Project Management from the University of Connecticut and a master’s degree in Accounting from Drexel University. Before attending Temple, Shuhua worked as an IT analyst at Louisiana State University for two and a half years.

Her research interests include health information technology, the social impact of IT, and digital platforms. She examines how technology adoption and public or platform-level policies influence outcomes at both the individual and societal levels, primarily using econometric methods.

Shuhua has taught and assisted in courses on data analytics and is committed to inclusive, student-centered pedagogy.
